# Chore-Ganizer User Guide

## Welcome to Chore-Ganizer! üè†

Chore-Ganizer is a family-friendly chore management app that helps families organize, track, and reward household chores.

## Getting Started

### Accessing the Application

1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to the application URL (e.g., `http://localhost:3002`)
3. You should see the login page

### Creating Your Account

If you don't have an account yet, a parent will need to create one for you. Ask a parent to:

1. Log in to their account
2. Go to **Users** section
3. Click **Add User**
4. Enter your details

### Logging In

1. Enter your email address
2. Enter your password
3. Click **Login**
4. You'll be taken to your dashboard

## Using the Dashboard

### Overview

Your dashboard shows:
- **Your Points:** Total points earned from completed chores
- **Assigned Chores:** Chores assigned to you
- **Recent Activity:** Your recent chore completions

### Viewing Your Chores

1. Click **My Chores** in the navigation menu
2. You'll see a list of all chores assigned to you
3. Each chore shows:
   - Chore name and description
   - Points value
   - Due date (if set)
   - Status (Pending, In Progress, Completed)

### Completing a Chore

1. Find the chore you want to complete
2. Click on the chore to view details
3. Click **Mark as Complete**
4. The points will be added to your total
5. A parent will be notified to approve the completion

### Tracking Points

1. View your total points on the dashboard
2. Click **Points History** to see:
   - Points earned from each chore
   - Date earned
   - Running total

## Understanding Chore Statuses

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Chore needs to be done |
| **In Progress** | You're working on it |
| **Completed** | You've finished the chore |
| **Approved** | Parent confirmed completion |
| **Overdue** | Past the due date |

## Tips for Success

1. **Check Daily:** Log in each day to see your assigned chores
2. **Communicate:** If you can't complete a chore, let a parent know
3. **Quality Matters:** Do chores well - parents approve completions
4. **Track Progress:** Watch your points grow over time
5. **Set Reminders:** Use the notification feature to get reminders

## Notifications

### Enabling Notifications

1. Click on your profile icon
2. Select **Notification Settings**
3. Enable the types of notifications you want:
   - New chore assignments
   - Chore reminders
   - Point awards
   - Approvals

### Viewing Notifications

1. Click the bell icon in the top right
2. See all your recent notifications
3. Click on a notification to view details

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I earn points?
Complete chores and have them approved by a parent. Each chore has a point value.

### Can I see my siblings' chores?
No, you can only see chores assigned to you. Parents can see all chores.

### What if I disagree with a point deduction?
Talk to your parent. They can adjust points as needed.

### How do I change my password?
Ask a parent to reset your password in the Users section.

### Can I suggest a new chore?
Yes! Talk to your parents about adding new chores to the system.

## Need Help?

If you have questions or issues:
1. Ask a parent for help
2. Check this guide again
3. Contact your family's app administrator
